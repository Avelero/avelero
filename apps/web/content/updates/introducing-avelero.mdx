## Our Mission

At Avelero, we're building the infrastructure for transparent supply chains. Our digital product passport platform helps fashion brands:

1. Calculate accurate environmental footprints
2. Create beautiful, branded product passports
3. Automate compliance with EU regulations
4. Build trust with conscious consumers
